# Notes on The Rearrangement and Density Cardinals
This repository contains the entire LaTeX project of my personal notes on Andreas Blass, Jörg Brendle, Will Brian, Joel David Hamkins, Michael Hardy, Paul B. Larson paper - The Rearrangement Number, and Christina Brech, Jörg Brendle, Márcio Telles paper - Density cardinals.

## Pdf File
The compiled version of these notes is available [here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Xennonio/The-Rearrangement-and-Density-Cardinals/main/The_Rearrangement_and_Density_Cardinals.pdf).

## About the Notes
These notes are merely a comprehensive and filtered summary of Andreas Blass, Jörg Brendle, Will Brian, Joel David Hamkins, Michael Hardy, Paul B. Larson paper - The Rearrangement Number; Christina Brech, Jörg Brendle, Márcio Telles paper - Density cardinals; and Andreas Blass chapter in Handbook of Set Theory - Combinatorial Cardinal Characteristics of the Continuum, along with some of my own intuitions that may be useful to someone, and some additional comments on things I've found elsewhere.

I will divide this document into three parts. The first part will explain the general theory of cardinal characteristics (still working on it) that we will use, the second part the rearrangement number, and the third part will cover the density cardinal.
